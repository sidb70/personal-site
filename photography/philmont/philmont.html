Philmont Scouting Reserve

In July 2019, I went to Philmont Scouting Reserve to embark on a 5 day backpacking trip in the New Mexican mountains. The crew was composed of 12 members from Troop 407, my local Boy Scout troop. The large rock in the distance in the second picture is called the Tooth of Time, which is also the summit featured in the next few slides.
